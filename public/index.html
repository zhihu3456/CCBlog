Climate, Air Quality Research (CARE) Unit

When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth.

Climate change is the most important science issue of the 21st century, challenging the very structure of our global society. We are a group of researchers who are passionate about understanding the climate change and its impact on human health. Our research is mainly based on Multi-Country Multi-City (MCC) Collaborative Research Network. Currently, we are focusing on the following research topics: landscape fire sourced-air pollution, tropical cyclone, floods and extreme weathers.
